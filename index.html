Famous Local Food

Baguio City, often called the “Summer Capital of the Philippines,” is famous for its sweet strawberries, creamy ube jam, and crisp, freshly harvested vegetables from nearby farms. Visitors can explore the vibrant Baguio Public Market, where colorful stalls overflow with local produce, pasalubong treats, and handmade crafts. As evening falls, the city comes alive with bustling night markets, offering an array of street food, souvenirs, and cozy mountain vibes that capture Baguio's unique charm.
